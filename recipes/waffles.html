Waffles

Description

Good old waffles. The never fail

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 3/4 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle iron
  2. Beat eggs
  3. Beat flour, milk, vegetable oil, sugar, baking powder, salt and vanilla
  4. Spray cooking spray and pour some mix
  5. Cook until brown
