Brenda's Lasagna

Description

This lasagna can be made in only an hour

Ingredients

  • 16 oz of lasagna noodles
  • 1lb beef
  • salt and pepper
  • 16oz spaghetti sauce
  • garlic
  • mozarella cheese
  • cheddar cheese
  • ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Boil ligthly salted water in a pot. Add and cook pasta for 10 minutes
  2. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C. Meanwhile, cook meat with salt and pepper
  3. Stir spaghetti sauce and garlic for 5 minutes
  4. Combine mozzarella, cheddar and ricotta
  5. In a pan alternate layers of noodles, meat and cheese until the pan is filled
  6. Bake in the oven until cheese in melted
