Bacon and Eggs Tacos

Description

Fast and delicios, these tacos won't let you down

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1/4 cup crumbled bacon
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 slices of american cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 6 tortillas
  • 1 cup of salsa

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs with bacon
  2. Mels butter, then add to egg mix
  3. Cook and stir egg mix.
  4. Stir cheese, salt and pepper
  5. Microwave tortillas for 30 seconds to 1 minute
  6. Spoon egg mix on every tortilla
